# The Lab

**AI Product Case Studies by Nikhil Patel**

Hands-on explorations at the intersection of data science, machine learning, and product strategy. Each project takes a real-world dataset or business problem, applies rigorous analysis, and delivers actionable strategic recommendations.

These case studies complement my enterprise product portfolio (Microsoft Azure, Oracle OCI, Citi, NextGen Technologies) by demonstrating the analytical and technical depth behind the product decisions.

## Case Studies
